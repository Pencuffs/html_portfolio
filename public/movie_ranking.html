Movies that you should not miss

The top 3 movies of all time for me

The Dark Knight

Just watch and admire the best villain ever who is just too attractive so that you unconsciously stand on his side. Fxxk Batman!
One of the first movies where the villain is way more attractive than the hero and does not defeat like all the stereotypical villains do.

Ratatoulle

I know that you will think: "An animation?"
But you won't be disappointed - like the motto in the movie, anything can be the best movie!

Eternal Sunshine of Spotless Mind

Jim Carrey does not only play funny characters!
The memory breakdown scene in the climax was one of the most touching moments.
